Emergency Response Procedure And Strategy


7 steps of initial response procedures

  1. Get out of the area

  2. Sound an alarm through the radio, alarm system or call out

  3. Assess the situation : consider other hazards and start the bug blower for ventilation (if

available)

  1. Protect yourself by donning a breathing apparatus

  2. If there are victims, rescue and bring them to a safe area

  3. Revive the victims: check victims' conditions and apply artificial respiration.

  4. Get medical aid

Contingency Plan

The following is a program of safety measures that plans for :

  1. Safety prevention and evacuation

  2. An action plan to be executed should there be a sudden release of H2S into the atmosphere

  3. Emergency procedure for evacuation of all personnel who might be endangered by the H2S

  4. First aid to injured workers

A contingency plan should be evaluated on a well-by-well basis. It is necessary for a well that can produce a concentration of H2S of 20 PPM or more to prepare a contingency plan.

The plan should be designed according to the well and location conditions, and should include safeguards for all personnel working on the drilling location and the people living in the surrounding area may be affected by the release of H2S gas.

Finally, the contingency plan must obey all Government and local tows regarding notification, precaution, evacuation procedures and aery other rules these bodies require. Make sure you read and understand the contingency plan that has been prepared for your work place.

Drilling Rigs Duties and Responsibilities

When H2S is detected, every effort will be made to ensure the safety of employees and the surrounding general public. To implement this contingency plan, a list of essential personnel has been compiled with individual duties and responsibilities.

The essential personnel will be responsible for taking necessary actions to control the well and reduce the H2S concentration, in addition to alerting operation and security personnel should conduct an evacuation if it is required.


Company-man Reps

Put on 30 SCBA, Proceed to rig floor to consider the situation and initiating a plan of action to control the well and operation.


Rig Superintendent

Put on 30 SCBA, Proceed to the rig floor and assess situation and take appropriate actions to control the well operation.


Tour Pusher

Put on 30 SCBA, responsible for do secure the well as notified by rig superintendent as well control procedure.


Driller

Put on 5 Ska Pak line, responsible for do secure the well as notified by rig superintendent as well control procedure.


Assistant Driller

Stand by with SCBA, ready to open choke if necessary.


Derrick-man

  • If on Monkev Board, put on 5 Ska Pak line, assist to Driller as well control procedure.

  • If on mud tank, assist to mud engineer.


Floor-man

Put on 5 Ska Pak Line, Assist to Driller do secure the well as well control procedures.



Mechanic

Stand by with 30 SCBA at Muster Point as Rescue Team, wait for further instruction from Safety Officer.


Electrician

Stand by with 30 SCBA at Muster Point as Rescue Team, wait for further instruction from Safety Officer.


Drilling Fluid Engineer

Stand by at Muster Point wait for further instruction from Company-man.


Mud Logger Team

Put on 10 SCBA then proceed to Designed Muster Point


Safety Officer

Proceed to Safe Briefing Area and must do a head count for all personnel. Make PA announcement for all non-essential personnel to assemble at Safe Briefing Area immediately. Notify Rescue Team if there is man down by H2S or other accident.


Paramedic/Doctor

Proceed to Safe Briefing Area with medical kit then make preparation for a rescue the victim.


Security

Road Block, changed with red flag, clear the way out to evacuation personal if necessary. Assist to Safety Officer.


Radio Operator

Opened all the door of camp site, and make sure no personal left, then proceed to muster point assist to Safety Officer.


H2S Safety Engineer

Put on 30 SCBA, Proceed to source H2S release & measure the gas with Gastec tube detector. Make sure that all non-essential personnel have assembled to Safe Briefing Area. Report the results to rig floor and Safety Officer at Safe Briefing Area. Continually monitor wind direction and report to rig floor and Master Point.
