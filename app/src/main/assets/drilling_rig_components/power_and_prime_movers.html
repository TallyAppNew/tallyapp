Power and Prime Movers

Internal combustion engine or a turbine that is the source of power for driving equipment on the Rig.

A drilling rig in which the source of power is one or more internal combustion engine and in which power is distributed to rig components through devices such as chains, sprockets, clutches and shaft is said to be a Mechanical Rig.

A drilling rig in which the source of power is effected by the combination of engines, generator set, control system and electric motors is said to be a Electric Drilling Rig.
